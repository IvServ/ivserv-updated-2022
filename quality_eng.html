Quality Engineering

What Is Cloud Automation?

Software quality engineering (SQE) is the application of QE practices to the software development lifecycle (SDLC). In software quality engineering, QE is closely integrated with existing agile and DevOps processes, as opposed to being added as a layer on top of existing workflows. This mirrors the shifted-left testing principles of DevSecOps teams and is intended to identify issues early on. In software quality engineering, engineers play a key role. Quality engineers design, implement, and maintain policies and procedures used to ensure the quality of development processes. This requires a thorough understanding of evaluation practices and technologies in use, including artificial intelligence (AI), big data analytics, and automation.

Primary Goals

Process control and oversight Implementing standards and metrics Data collection and analysis Test development Identification of issues and solutions Follow-up to ensure corrective actions
